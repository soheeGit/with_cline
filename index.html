About Kim Beom-soo

Kim Beom-soo is a South Korean internet entrepreneur who founded Kakao, one of the most successful tech companies in South Korea. He is known for his innovative approach to technology and his ability to identify and capitalize on emerging trends in the digital landscape.

Career Timeline

  • 1998: Founded Hangame, an online game portal
  • 2006: Founded Kakao Corp (formerly known as I-WILAB)
  • 2010: Launched KakaoTalk, a free mobile messaging app
  • 2014: Kakao merged with Daum Communications, becoming Daum Kakao
  • 2015: Daum Kakao rebranded as Kakao Corp
  • Present: Kakao has expanded into various sectors, including fintech, mobility, and content

Achievements

KakaoTalk

Revolutionized mobile communication in South Korea with over 50 million active users.

KakaoPay
